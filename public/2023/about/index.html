Bio

I'm a designer, developer, and teacher. I spent two years in the Peace Corps teaching math and computer skills in Tanzania, and I'm always looking for work that connects to education and expanding opportunity. I've worked in nonprofits, academia, and corporate environments, but the projects I care most about are the ones that help people and strive to make the world a better place.

I currently design products that help users manage and pay down their student debt at Fidelity Investments. Sometimes, I teach design and tech classes as an adjunct instructor. I also co-run a mini design studio called Best Parts.

In my free time, I make music, post on youtube, watch movies, go on long runs, and explore the great outdoors.

About this site

This site is a portfolio and archive for my designs, art, and music.

I built it with Astro JS and Tailwind CSS. It is also hosted on github. It is open source, meaning you are free to clone it and reuse any part of it.
