Imagine Public Transportation

Overview
Billboard designs that explore the costs of car-centric cities, and the benefits of public transportation. The designs aim to get people thinking about how we can build more livable cities for everyone.

Problem

A publishing class I took in grad school forced me to reconsider what makes something a publication. During one project, I explored how a public medium might "reach across the aisle" and create discussion around urban planning in the United States. I designed a series of billboards with a slightly sarcastic tone.
