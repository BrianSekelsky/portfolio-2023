Greenways.com

Overview
Elizabeth and I (as Best Parts) were hired by Chuck Flink to re-design his website, Greenways.com. Chuck Flink is an accomplished landscape architect and greenways consultant. He wanted a new website to catalog his work over the years and serve as a hub for new clients and collaborators to find him.

Organization and content management

We built Chuck’s site on WordPress so he can update content himself. A self-service CMS ensures he won’t need developer support just to add a new project or tweak copy.

Homepage built on top of Wordpress

Sitemap overhaul

The old structure was deep and confusing. We flattened the hierarchy around Chuck’s core services, making it easier for visitors to find what they need in one click.

Original sitemap

Revised sitemap

Visual refresh

Chuck’s brand needed a modern, clean aesthetic that still felt rooted in nature. We introduced a light palette, clear typography, and generous white space to foreground his portfolio.

Before

After

Custom project illustrations

Elizabeth created a bespoke botanical illustration for each of Chuck’s five highlighted projects. Each drawing features a plant native to its region, grounding the site in real landscapes and adding a layer of local storytelling.

Highlighting expertise

We carved out a dedicated intro section on the homepage to spotlight Chuck’s credentials and past projects.

Feature: Expert profile

Clarifying offerings

Chuck’s services can seem specialized at first glance. We created a scannable offerings grid that breaks down each service into plain-language headings and short descriptions.

Interactive service breakdown

Responsiveness

The legacy site was desktop-only. We rebuilt every component to adapt fluidly from desktop to tablet and mobile, this ensures touch targets, layout, and typography scale seamlessly.

Mobile view

Final reflection

Chuck Flink's new website is an organized repository for all of his professional achievements. New clients are able to quickly get in contact with him and find important information about him with ease.
