Winter Foe
Winter Foe is a music project that I have been working on since around 2018. I am searching for new ways to combine digital sounds, electronics, acoustic instruments, and field recordings. Through this project I have expanded my visual vocabulary by producing album art, animations, and videos. I have also had the opportunity to collaborate with some amazing musicians.
